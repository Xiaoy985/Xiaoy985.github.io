My New Puppy

If you decide to own a puppy, think carefully ! ! !

During the weekend I picked a puppy from the breeder with my roommate and it was a Cavapoo!!

We named him Pupu, and He was only three months old, with curly brown and white fur.

When choosing among a group of dogs, we saw him at first sight! I was instantly captured by his cute expression and decided it was him!
